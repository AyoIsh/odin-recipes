Coke Recipe

A refreshing glass of coke to give you a boost of energy!

I like to have it with my lunch it's a great pick me up.

Ingredients

  • Water
  • Soda Stream
  • Coke flavoring also cocaine

Instructions

  1. Use the soda stream on an 8oz glass of water
  2. Pour in the coke flavoring and cocaine
  3. Enjoy!
